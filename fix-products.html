🔧 Fix Product Tracking

Link Your Existing Products

This will link your previously submitted products to your account so they appear in your Account Dashboard.

Step 1: Make sure you're logged in to your account
Step 2: Click the button below to link your products
Step 3: Go to Account Dashboard → Listed Products to see them

What This Does:

  • Finds products you submitted using your name, email, or phone
  • Links them to your user account
  • Makes them appear in your Account Dashboard
  • Doesn't affect the products themselves, just the ownership
